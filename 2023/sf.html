Sagrada Familia

Gaudí‘s tour de force is breathtaking. With its interior designed like a forest, it is a mesmerising, monumental undertaking, blending architecture, art, and engineering into perfection — a true devotion.

We stepped into Sagrada Família last week to celebrate my daughter’s graduation. Standing beneath the starry ceiling, we let its magnificence wash over us.
