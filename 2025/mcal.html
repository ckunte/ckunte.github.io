Med school calendar

Kid’s year starts-off with post-grad sem 1 of Med school — a 20 week programme, and it is packed. While bush-walking in Yanchep National Park earlier this week, she said the school chose to provide a pdf version of the time table, but she’s interested in importing it into either Google Calendar or Notion. Google Calendar offers to import a CSV file, but creating it with hundreds of items is grunt work.

Creating a new calendar for this task, and testing the import shows the entries as expected. So the work is cut out to first populate a CSV file before the whole sem 1 programme can be imported. She’s busy with it now. (While Google Gemini AI can take-in a screenshot and turn it into a Google Calendar specific CSV, we’re not uploading school calendar to it.)
