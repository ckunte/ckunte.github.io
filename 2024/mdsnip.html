Markdown snippets

30 Nov 2024, 1 min

After switching to Vim, I needed two snippets to be able to write without pausing to add mark-ups for links, and figures. They are as below, and now available from GitHub.

snippet lnk "Link" b
[${1:key}]: ${2:url} "${3:title}"
endsnippet

snippet fig "Figure" b
<figure>
  <img alt="${1:description}" src="${2:url}" loading=lazy />
  <figcaption>${3:caption}</figcaption>
</figure>
endsnippet

Assuming Vim Plug for managing plug-ins, one would add this below to ~/.vimrc, and run :PlugUpdate in the Normal mode to get it working.

call plug#begin()

Plug 'sirver/ultisnips'
Plug 'ckunte/markdown-snippets-vim'

call plug#end()

let g:UltiSnipsExpandTrigger = '<tab>'
let g:UltiSnipsJumpForwardTrigger = '<c-j>'
let g:UltiSnipsJumpBackwardTrigger = '<c-k>'
